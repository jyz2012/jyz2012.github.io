About alien invasion

by Jyz2012
Welcome to Alien Invasion!

In this game, you will become the last line of defense of the earth and face waves of alien invasions. Your mission is to pilot the spaceships, shoot bullets, defend the planet, and get the highest score possible.

Control the ship to move left and right and shoot bullets to defeat the oncoming aliens. The alien will move faster and faster, and you'll need to take the opportunity to kill it.

Destroying aliens will earn you points, and destroying a wave of aliens will upgrade your ship's weapons. Upgraded weapons can fire bullets more quickly. You have three spaceships, and the game is over when all three ships are destroyed.

You can use the left and right buttons to control the direction and shoot with a spacebar.

Good luck and have fun!
